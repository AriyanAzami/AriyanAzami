### 🧑‍💻 About me

I'm a Computer Engineering student at York University with a soft spot for building things that combine **smart software** with **real hardware** — medical-imaging models, computer-vision pipelines, custom drones, and award-winning robots. Currently a Software Developer at [Appli Solutions](https://applisolutions.com), an AI-powered hiring platform, and a Machine Learning Research Assistant at York's Laboratory of Advanced Biotechnologies.

---

### 🔭 What I'm working on right now

- 🧠 **Medical imaging with deep learning** — training U-Net segmentation models and exploring Stable Diffusion–based generative pipelines for diagnostic imaging at York's Laboratory of Advanced Biotechnologies.
- 🤖 **Computer vision for autonomous systems** — co-developed a real-time obstacle-detection dataset and YOLO model, released open-source on [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/abtinzandi/obstacle-detection-dataset) and [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Abtinz/Obstacle-Detection-Dataset-YOLO).
- 💻 **Production software** — shipping backend and frontend features at Appli Solutions across the full stack.
- 🚁 **Custom quadcopters** — building and tuning drones with ArduPilot firmware and DJI flight controllers, learning PID loops the hard way.
